Skaters Club

Site Name

The site name chosen for this project is "Urban Skate Alliance". This name reflects the mission of the club, which is to unite urban skaters from diverse backgrounds and skill levels, fostering a sense of community and promoting the art and culture of skating in urban environments.

Optional domain availability: urbanskatealliance.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Skaters Club website is to serve as a central platform for skating enthusiasts to connect, share resources, and stay updated on the latest trends and events in the skating community.

  • * The site provides a forum for skaters of all levels to interact and share tips, tricks, and experiences.
  • * It offers comprehensive guides on various skateboarding disciplines, including street, vert, and longboarding.
  • * Users can find information on local skate parks, events, and competitions, as well as participate in group outings and meetups.
  • * Additionally, the website features exclusive interviews with professional skaters, gear reviews, and tutorials to help members improve their skills.

Scenarios

  • * What are the best skateboards for beginners looking to learn basic tricks?
  • * Where can I find information about upcoming skateboarding events and competitions in my city?

Color Schema

The selected color scheme for the website includes:

  • Primary Color: #28a745 (Green)
  • * Headings: This vibrant green color will be used for headings such as page titles, section titles, and article titles to make them stand out.
  • * Used for buttons, links, and other interactive elements to draw attention and encourage engagement.
  • Secondary Color: Bisque (used for backgrounds and paragraphs)
  • * Background: Bisque, being a soft and neutral color, will be used as the background color for the website to provide a clean and calming backdrop for content.
  • * Paragraphs: Bisque will also be used for paragraphs and body text to ensure readability and maintain a cohesive visual style throughout the site.

Typography

The following fonts will be used:

  • Heading Font: Arial, sans-serif
