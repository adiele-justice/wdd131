History

Urban Skate Alliance was founded in the year 1998 by four enthusiasts: Mark Johnson, Sarah Rodriguez, David Thompson, and Emily Lee. Since its inception, the alliance has been a hub for skateboarders, fostering community and passion for the sport. It has endured for over two decades, evolving with the skating culture and remaining a cornerstone of the urban skateboarding scene.
